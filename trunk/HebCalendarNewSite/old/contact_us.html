contact us // mailing list

If you would like to contact Cluster Q about anything, please e-mail one of our club officers. If your enquiry covers one of the categories mentioned below, some contacts are suggested to help you route your enquiry effectively:

Sponsorship - please route all enquiries via the Club Presidents
Recruiting - please route all enquiries via the VP of Careers
Prospective Students - please route all enquiries via the VP of Outreach and Recruiting
Non-profits and other LGBT organizations - please route all enquiries via the VP of Community
Other Columbia Schools - please route all enquiries via the VP of Community
